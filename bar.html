Bar & Back Bar Area

18′ × 10′ · West wall · 7 stools

Back to Floor Plan

Overview

The bar runs the full length of the west wall — a 20-foot front bar facing seven stools, with a 20-foot back-bar working wall behind the bartender. The front bar carries seven equipment slots (hand sink, wine preservation, 48″ wine fridge, ice bin, glass froster, 3-comp sink, glasswasher) plus two millwork slots for clean landing and stem storage. The back bar carries the coffee station, hot-water dispenser, induction burner, a 140″ marble pastry slab for chocolate-flight plating and cocktail prep, and two reach-in refrigerators (cocktail mise + wine backup; reds at 58°F).

The bar program is built around a wine-by-the-glass dispenser as the operational center of gravity. The Wineemotion OTTO 8-bottle preservation system on the front bar runs the BTG list; the 48″ Perlick HC48RW fridge stages sparkling and white/rosé backup at service temperature; the back-bar reach-in holds reds at 58°F. Together those three pieces of equipment hold roughly 50–60 bottles of working inventory at the bar, with the rest of the program housed in the wine display and back-of-house storage.

Bar layout and equipment were reviewed on May 11, 2026 against the May 9 NOVUS BOH layout. The review concluded the bar requires no equipment deletions, one use-case change on the front bar (Slot 3 fridge moves entirely to wine service), and two use-case changes on the back bar (Reach-in #1 to cocktail mise + wine backup; French press added to coffee station for Sunday brunch). Those changes are reflected in the current spec.
