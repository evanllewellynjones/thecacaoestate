Polycarbonate Molds & Smallwares

Slot 19 · Custom CC-monogrammed signature molds + standard mold library + chocolatier smallwares

Back to Chocolate Production

Overview

Vol. II §8 specifies the mold strategy: custom polycarbonate molds with the CC monogram debossed at center, three shape categories (square = dark, dome = milk, round = white) with two of each shape for parallel production, plus a broader inventory of standard molds for seasonal varieties and limited editions. The full opening kit at 50+ molds covers the signature six-piece line plus a working library for seasonal rotations and any class/event production.

Lead Times & Timing Lock in the custom signature molds 8 weeks before opening (lead time + 2-week buffer). Standard molds and smallwares can be ordered 2–3 weeks before opening once the chocolatier is on board and can spec their preferences. Total Slot 19 budget is $3,400–6,800; the $2,500–4,500 tighter version is likely sufficient if you skew toward fewer standard molds at opening and let the chocolatier expand the library in year 1.

Slot 19a — Custom CC-Monogrammed Signature Molds

Custom polycarbonate molds — signature line

Slot 19a
Stored on prep-table undershelf and reach-in shelves · Polycarbonate, food-safe · CC monogram debossed at piece center
Recommended — Tomric Systems custom polycarbonate molds (US, 4–6 week lead time)
ModelCustom CC-debossed molds: square (dark), dome (milk), small round (white) — 2 each, 6 total signature molds · alternative: Chocolate World custom (Belgium, 6–8 week lead)
Price (est.)$300–600 per custom shape · 6 signature molds total: $1,800–3,600
DimensionsStandard polycarbonate mold size: 275 × 175 mm (~10.8″ × 6.9″), 21 cavities per mold at 1.25″ piece size. Custom debossing adds ~$150–300 per shape one-time tooling charge, amortized into the mold price.
Why this oneCustom debossing is the brand investment — every piece that leaves the case carries the CC monogram, which is what makes the chocolate visually identifiable on the boards and in the retail box. Tomric is the closer (US) supplier and integrates with your other Tomric purchases (vibration table, tempering machine). Order 2 of each shape for parallel workflow — while one mold sets, the other is being filled. Lead time is 4–8 weeks so order at the same time as the marble fabrication.

Order Timing & Coordination

  • Custom molds: order 8 weeks before opening to absorb 4–6 week lead time plus 2-week buffer. Coordinate with marble work table order (both have ~4–6 week leads) so they arrive in the same window for prep room build-out.
  • Standard molds + smallwares: defer until 2–3 weeks before opening so the hired chocolatier can spec their preferences. Order arrives within 7–10 business days from WebstaurantStore, Tomric, or restaurant supply.
  • Daily production target: one mold per active variety per day plus reserve in display case (per Vol. II §8). Two molds per shape supports parallel workflow — one fills while the other sets.
